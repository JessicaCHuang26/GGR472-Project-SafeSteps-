SafeSteps Toronto

Data Sources

  • Crime incidents — Toronto Police Service Open Data (2022)
  • Neighbourhood crime rates — City of Toronto Open Data
  • Police facility locations — City of Toronto Open Data
  • Routing — Mapbox Directions API (walking profile)

Team

GGR472 Group Project — University of Toronto

How to Use

  1. Enter a start location or tap 📍 to use your GPS
  2. Enter a destination or choose a popular landmark
  3. Choose Both, Fastest, or Safest to filter routes
  4. Hover over any neighbourhood to see its crime risk level
  5. Use the Filter by Risk slider to highlight high-risk areas
